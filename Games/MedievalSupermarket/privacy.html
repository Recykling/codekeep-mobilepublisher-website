Privacy Policy for "Medieval Supermarket" Mobile Game

Last updated: 04.12.2023

Thank you for using the "Medieval Supermarket" app. This privacy policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data while using our game.

1. Collection of Information

1.1. Non-personal Data

The "Medieval Supermarket" app does not collect any personal or identifiable information. We do not gather any data that could be used to identify users.

1.2. Advertisements

To provide the game for free, we utilize advertising services such as AdMob and Unity. These ads may collect general usage data, but they do not contain personally identifiable information.

2. Use of Information

None of the information collected is used to identify users. Data collected by advertisements is used solely for statistical purposes and to optimize the game's performance.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share any collected personal data with third parties. Information collected by advertisements is shared with advertising service providers solely for the purpose of delivering advertising services within the game.

4. Security

We employ appropriate security measures to protect the information collected by our application from unauthorized access, loss, disclosure, or modification.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update our privacy policy to reflect changes in our privacy protection practices. These updates will be posted on our website or within the game itself.

6. Contact

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at bearmindeddev@gmail.com.
